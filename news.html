Why a News Page?

Research-group websites need visible activity, not only static pages.

News items help visitors understand what is changing: new project directions, internship openings, publication milestones, development releases, team growth, workshops, and collaboration calls. This page turns those updates into a searchable and filterable knowledge stream.

Time-Aware

Filterable
