Team Philosophy

A research group should show not only people, but also roles, outputs, and contribution pathways.

The HUMACH team page is structured to support current members and future contributors. It separates leadership, research, development, internship, collaboration, and alumni roles so the website can grow without redesigning the page every time a new contributor joins.

Role-aware

Profiles identify how each contributor participates in research, development, training, or collaboration.

Connected

Team members can later connect to projects, research areas, outputs, and publications.

Output-driven

Contributor cards highlight visible outputs such as reports, code, figures, prototypes, and papers.

Growth-ready

The page supports future interns, students, alumni, and external collaborators.

Contribution Pathways

Team profiles should eventually connect to every major HUMACH output.

As the website grows, each member can be linked to their projects, research areas, publications, technical outputs, and internship milestones.

01

Research contribution

Literature reviews, experiments, datasets, model evaluation, and publication support.

02

Development contribution

Code, dashboards, UI modules, data loaders, documentation, and software prototypes.

03

Training contribution

Internship milestones, student projects, thesis supervision, and portfolio-ready evidence.

04

Collaboration contribution

External research ideas, institutional partnerships, technical mentoring, and applied impact.
