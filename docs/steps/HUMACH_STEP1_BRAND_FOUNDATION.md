# HUMACH Research — Step 1 Brand Foundation Implementation

## Implemented Step

**Step #1: Foundation and Brand Setup**

This step creates the visual and textual foundation for the redesigned HUMACH Research website before building the full set of pages.

---

## Locked Brand Identity

**Short Name:** HUMACH Research

**Full Name:** Human-centered Ubiquitous Machine intelligence for Advanced Computing and Health

**Brand Positioning:** A computer-science research and development group focused on human-centered AI, ubiquitous machine intelligence, advanced computing, health technology, training, internships, and collaboration.

---

## What Changed

### 1. Global Brand Wording

Updated the main homepage and site identity to use:

```text
HUMACH Research
Human-centered Ubiquitous Machine intelligence for Advanced Computing and Health
```

The hero section now clearly introduces HUMACH as a research, development, training, and collaboration platform.

---

### 2. Header / Navigation Foundation

The header was updated from a generic template-style navigation to a HUMACH-branded navigation.

Current navigation items:

```text
Home
Identity
Pillars
Focus Areas
Collaborate
Join HUMACH
```

These are temporary Step 1 anchors. Future steps will replace or expand them with full pages such as Research, Development, Projects, Team, Publications, Opportunities, News, and Contact.

---

### 3. Visual Theme Foundation

A new dedicated stylesheet was added:

```text
assets/css/humach-theme.css
```

This file contains the HUMACH-specific design system and overrides the original generic template styling.

The design direction is:

```text
Futuristic academic research style
Dark navy foundation
Cyan, green, and violet accent gradients
Glassmorphism panels
Research-network visual feel
Modern rounded cards
Professional but innovative typography
```

---

### 4. Typography

The site now uses a more modern typography pairing:

```text
Inter — body and navigation
Space Grotesk — headings and brand display text
```

These fonts make the site feel more suitable for AI, computing, research, and innovation.

---

### 5. Hero Section Foundation

The new hero section includes:

```text
HUMACH Research
Human-centered Ubiquitous Machine intelligence for Advanced Computing and Health
Research · Development · Training · Collaboration
```

It also includes two call-to-action buttons:


### 6. Brand Identity Section

A new identity section explains what HUMACH means and why the name fits the research group.

It emphasizes:

```text
Human-centered AI
Ubiquitous intelligence
Research-to-development direction
Health and advanced computing relevance
```

### 7. Core Website Pillars

A new pillar section introduces the future structure of the site:

```text

```

These will guide the next pages and content architecture.

---

### 8. Focus Areas Refresh

The previous “Research Scopes” section was visually refreshed and renamed to:


### 9. Footer Foundation

The footer was changed from a simple copyright-only footer to a HUMACH-branded footer with:
